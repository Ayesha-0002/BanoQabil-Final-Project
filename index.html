Welcome to Bano Qabil

Bano Qabil is a free, youth-focused educational initiative by the Alkhidmat Foundation that provides advanced IT and digital skills training to help young people in Pakistan launch their careers. The program offers courses in high-demand fields like web development, graphic design, and digital marketing to empower individuals to find employment or start freelancing.

Purpose is to To empower the youth of Pakistan by providing them with free, advanced IT skills for career development and to contribute to the country's economic growth.The program is open to all youth, regardless of their background, with a specific focus on inclusivity for women and underprivileged students.Graduates are equipped with practical skills to either find jobs with partner companies, start their own businesses, or succeed as freelancers.

Our mission is to provide underprivileged students and women with **industry-relevant IT training**, helping them secure employment, start freelancing, and build a sustainable tech career.
